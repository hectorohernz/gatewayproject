Parks

Mount Yoshino

Nara, Japan

Mount Yoshino is a historical location. Yoshino is Japan's most famous cherry blooms location. Visitors can a enjoy a eye pleasing picture from Mount Yoshino. It has been around for about 1300 Years.

Disneyland

Chiba, Japan

Disneyland is a awesome location, where anyone can be a playful child. It's a great environment for any type of person, they have a several different types of rides. This Disneyland location was the first of many international locations, and is the largest international location

Mount Fuji

Honsho, Japan

Mount fuji is the second highest volcano in Asia. Mount Fuji is one of Japan's "Three Holy Mountains" along with Mount Tate and Mount Haku. It is also a Special Place of Scenic Beauty and one of Japan's Historic Sites. This park is targeted to everyone, including pets.

Museums

The Sumida Hokusai Museum

Tokyo, Japan

Sumbida Hokusai is a museum dedicated to modern art and which includes high-quality replicas of Hokusai's works, including his woodcut cycle. Sumbia has different types of displays, which range from Anime to Sports art. This museum is targeted towards a adult.

Ueno Zoo

Tokyo, Japan

Ueno Zoo is the oldest zoo location in Japan. A large urban zoo that is home to a zoo and a small amusement park, which is located inside the zoo.

Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation

Tokyo, Japan

The National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation, simply known as the Miraikan, is a museum created by Japan's Science and Technology Agency. It was opened in 2001.
